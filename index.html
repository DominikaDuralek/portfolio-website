I'm a recent IT graduate based in Poland. I'm creative, well-organised and hard-working individual with a perfectionist side. I fell in love with programming, because it allows me to create things from scratch. Seeing lines of code turn into something that works is one of my biggest joys in life. I'm an artist and I treat coding as a form of art. From carefully planning a project, through development to a finished product, I enjoy every step of the process.

I graduated from Politechnika Bydgoska in 2024, specialized in Information Technologies. My university experience allowed me to learn my basics and get interested in programming. I took part in many student projects and an IT internship. For my final project I designed and developed a mobile app with minigames, made with Unity game engine. I received the highest grade on my diploma. After graduation I decided to take up web development.
