I'm a recent IT graduate based in Poland. I'm a creative, extremely well-organised and hard-working individual with a perfectionist side.

My love for programming stems from it allowing me to create things from scratch. Seeing lines of code turn into something that works is one of my biggest joys in life.

I'm an artist and I treat coding as a form of art. From carefully planning my projects, through development to finished products, I enjoy every step of the process.

I graduated from Politechnika Bydgoska in 2024, specializing in Information Technologies. My university experience equipped me with a strong foundation and sparked my interest in programming.

During my studies, I actively participated in numerous student projects and completed an IT internship. For my Engineer's Thesis, I designed and developed a mobile app featuring minigames using the Unity game engine, earning the highest grade on my diploma.

Following graduation, I made the decision to pursue web development. Currently, I am focused on creating independent projects to further refine and expand my skill set.
